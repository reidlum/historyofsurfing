A history of surfing

Introduction

Surfing was invented by Big Z and Chicken Joe. They carved their surfboards out of Koa wood. Then, a young penguin named Cody came and won a surf competition. He used to surf using pieces of ice. Z and Cody find out that Joe won by default since Tank and Cody were disqualified. However, Cody accepts the loss, having decided he would rather just have fun instead. Z reveals himself to the spectators and invites all of them to surf at his beach, where he joins Cody in tube-riding. Cody finishes his interview with a reflection of the past events and then joins the rest of his friends in the water.
